About Me

My name is Alexandr Peters but I go by Sasha. Here's a little about me as a person. I was adopted from Russia in 1998 at the age of 4. I've played sports most of my life including swimming, cross country, and my favorite sport soccer. In my junior year of highschool my soccer team won the state championship for our division.

I have a passion for programming and solving problems. I graduated from Sewanee, The University of the South in May 2018 with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. I am currently enrolled in the coding bootcamp at Vanderbilt University to broaden my understanding and confidence in fullstack webdevelopment. I am excited to see what this opportunity teaches me.
